# TriageAI: 6-Minute Investor Pitch Video Script

## I. Overall Video Production & Delivery Guidance

*   **Pacing:** Keep a brisk, energetic pace without rushing. You have a lot to cover in 6 minutes, so every second counts.
*   **Tone:** Be confident, passionate, and professional. You're not just presenting a project; you're selling a vision.
*   **Recording Environment:**
    *   **Audio:** Use a high-quality microphone. Poor audio is a major distraction. Record in a quiet room to avoid background noise.
    *   **Lighting:** Ensure your face is well-lit from the front. Avoid backlighting (e.g., sitting with a window behind you).
    *   **Background:** Use a clean, professional background. It could be a simple wall, a bookshelf, or a virtual background with the TriageAI logo.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Mix it up:** Combine talking head shots with screen recordings, animations, and on-screen text to keep the video engaging.
    *   **High-Quality Screen Recordings:** Record your prototype in high resolution. Use a tool that can highlight clicks and mouse movements.
    *   **On-Screen Text:** Use bold, easy-to-read fonts. Animate text to appear as you mention the key points.
*   **Editing:**
    *   **Cut between shots:** Switch between the talking head and the demo to maintain viewer interest.
    *   **Add background music:** A subtle, professional background track can enhance the video's feel.
    *   **Practice, practice, practice:** Rehearse the script multiple times to ensure a smooth, natural delivery.
